Allen Kubai

Allen is Kenyan, 30 years old, lives in Nairobi and is the Director of Technology. He is responsible for the Design & Development of Tunaweza System and for the unique Tunaweza Apprentice Program

Personal message

I joined JJPeople in 2008 after graduation from JKU with a degree in Computer Science and worked as a Java Developer. In 2010 I was appointed to lead a team of four developers for Learn Direct who are based in Sheffield in English Midlands. I spent several months in that lovely town working with some pretty cool guys then in 2012 I was given a second team to lead this time for Pixelboxx GmbH who are based in Dortmund, Germany. My team and I worked on their advanced asset management system and I enjoyed working with two of their senior developers who came to Kenya for a few months. Then, late in 2013 I joined the Tunaweza as Technical Director where I set up the team that wrote the Tunaweza system. Tunaweza is a Kenyan company, very different sort of company to anything I have known before. First of all we are managed by developers. Tunaweza is not focused on the bottom line and where other companies value profit we value ‘values’. We believe in principal over profit, in community over corporate interest and we believe that knowledge is a resource to be shared not a product to be sold. We do not do it this way for fun – although it is great fun – we do it because it produces spectacular results.
